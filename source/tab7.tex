\begin{tabular}{|l|l|}\hline
Key      &  Action\\ \hline
   SPACE & Fire starter on selected engine(s)\\
   !     & Select 1st engine\\
   @		 & Select 2nd engine\\
  \#     & Select 3rd engine\\
  \$     & Select 4th engine\\
  \{     & Decrease Magneto on Selected Engine\\
  \}     & Increase Magneto on Selected Engine\\
  $\sim$   & Select all Engines\\\hline
\end{tabular}
